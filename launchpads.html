Launchpad: 6 Weeks to Launch Your Startup Project

If you have an idea you can't stop thinking about, our Launchpad program helps you turn it into something tangible. Each week, we offer mentorship, workshops, and a final Demo Day to launch your startup project with founders, potential investors, and our engineering community.

Apply to Launchpad 2025

Timeline (4/17 - 5/29 , every thursday)

Program Highlights


Week 1-2: Ideation & Team Formation
Brainstorming: Learn methods like mind mapping and design thinking to shape your concept. Team Building: Meet other participants, form teams based on shared interests, and outline a plan. Kickoff Workshop: Gain insights into market validation, user research, and setting clear goals.



Week 3-5: Product Development
Technical Focus: Start coding, prototyping, or designing your solution with weekly mentor check-ins. Iterative Testing: Gather feedback from peers and advisors to refine features as you go. Midpoint Workshop: Delve into best practices for product design, usability, and iteration.



Week 6-7: Business & Pitch Prep
Business Model: Work through the basics of a go-to-market plan, pricing, and customer outreach. Pitch Deck Crafting: Learn how to tell your story, highlight your unique value, and structure your slides. Practice Sessions: Rehearse in front of mentors, get feedback, and fine-tune delivery.



Week 8: Demo Day
Pitch to Investors & VCs: Present your polished product to a panel of potential funders, community leaders, and other students. Showcase Event: Celebrate your journey, share lessons learned, and expand your network. Next Steps: Get advice on securing funding, seeking partnership opportunities, or continuing development after Launchpad.
