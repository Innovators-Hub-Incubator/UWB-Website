Launchpad

6 Weeks to Launch Your Startup Project

If you have an idea you can't stop thinking about, our Launchpad program helps you turn it into something tangible. Each week, we offer mentorship, workshops, and a final Demo Day to launch your startup project with founders, potential investors, and our engineering community.

Apply to Launchpad 2025

Timeline & Highlights

Week 1: Ideation

  • Kickoff workshop: market validation, user research, goal setting
  • Brainstorm with mind mapping & design thinking

Week 2: Team Formation

  • Meet other participants, form teams, outline a plan
  • Team-building activities and networking

Week 3: Prototyping

  • Start coding, prototyping, or designing your solution
  • Mentor check-ins & feedback from peers

Week 4: Iteration

  • Iterative testing: gather feedback, refine features
  • Midpoint workshop: product design, usability, iteration

Week 5: Business & Pitch Prep

  • Go-to-market plan, pricing, customer outreach
  • Pitch deck crafting: story, value, slides
  • Practice sessions: feedback & delivery

Demo Day

  • Pitch to investors, VCs, and community leaders
  • Showcase event: celebrate, share, network
  • Next steps: funding, partnerships, development
Note: Spaces fill up quickly. Apply above!
